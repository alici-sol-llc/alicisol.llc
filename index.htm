Biography

Alici (Alice V. de M. Sol) (born 28 January 1999) is a Brazilian-American singer, songwriter, and performer. She composes and performs mainly jazz songs while also having techno songs published as Girasol.

Born in Belo Horizonte, Minas Gerais, Brazil, she grew up in Seattle, WA, while living for 3.5 years in Cambridge-UK. In 2021, she received a Bachelor of Music in Songwriting from the Berklee College of Music in Boston, MA, and moved to New York City, where she resides.
